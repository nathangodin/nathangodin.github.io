## Buy vs. Rent Calculator

An interactive map-based tool that compares the true total cost of buying versus renting at the ZIP code level across San Francisco, New York City, and Washington DC metro areas, with historical analysis from 2000 to 2026.

Unlike simple mortgage calculators, this tool models the full financial picture over time by computing the net worth of two scenarios: (1) buying a home and building equity, versus (2) renting and investing the down payment and monthly savings across a range of investment strategies (S&P 500, 80/20, 60/40, treasuries, HYSA, etc.).

**Key features:**

- **ZIP-level interactive map** showing breakeven horizons across 1,300+ ZIP codes, including suburban areas (Bay Area, NJ, VA/MD suburbs)
- **Detailed cost modeling** — mortgage payments (conforming vs. jumbo), property taxes (city-specific rules including Prop 13 and NYC tax classes), HOA/condo fees calibrated from REIT 10-K filings and AHS microdata, maintenance, and utilities
- **Per-ZIP insurance estimates** using NAIC state averages, NFIP flood premiums, CAL FIRE wildfire risk zones, and California Earthquake Authority rates
- **Tax benefits** — mortgage interest deduction, SALT cap, capital gains exclusion, state capital gains taxes, and NIIT
- **Rent regulation detection** for NYC (rent-stabilized buildings, 421-a, J-51, HDFC, Section 8, Mitchell-Lama)
- **FEMA flood zone overlay** and fire risk indicators
- **Advanced settings** — credit score adjustments, filing status, property type (condo/co-op/townhouse/single-family), investment strategy, and refinancing logic

All data sourced from public records and government APIs.
